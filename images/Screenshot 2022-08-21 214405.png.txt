A MORNING
PRAYER

Der nod,

Thork pou for this beautiful dey.

Thank pou far ghing me the chance

fo see this doy

Thank you for loving me decpite sry sine
Forgive me for all nny sins ewer the ones
commited unknowingly.

Ag! stort thes doy, # pray Far your
guidanes and your prodesiien.

Direct nese path ved Aeach re tie

righ? thing le de.

Let my words and my anions be onec
Ahot being horgue fe your Holy Marne.
Thank you Deve, Fer an anewered prayer,
Thrauigih desut Christ! pray

Arun
